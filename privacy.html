Privacy Standards 2024

Privacy Policy

At Giftpose, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across all our services and compliance tools.

Secure by Design

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, following military-grade protocols.

Full Transparency

We never sell your data to third parties. You have total control over your information.

Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, use our legal documentation tools, or communicate with our support team. This includes:

  • Personal identifiers such as name and email address.
  • Organization details including jurisdiction and compliance requirements.
  • Financial information for subscription billing and transaction history.

Use of Data

Giftpose uses the collected data for various professional purposes to ensure the highest quality of service and legal accuracy:

Service Provision

To provide and maintain our Service, including monitoring usage patterns.

Communication

To contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials.

Compliance

To detect, prevent and address technical issues or legal discrepancies.

Optimization

To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the platform.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Type Purpose
Session Cookies We use these to operate our Service efficiently.
Preference Cookies We use these to remember your settings and preferences.
Security Cookies We use these for security purposes and fraud detection.

Your Rights

Giftpose aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:

  • 1 Right to access, update or delete the information we have.
  • 2 Right of rectification if information is inaccurate.
  • 3 Right to object to our processing of your Personal Data.
